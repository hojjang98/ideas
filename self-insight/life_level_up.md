This project proposes a mobile application designed to combat low motivation, a lack of clear life goals, and general unhappiness by transforming personal development into a **gamified quest system**. The core mechanism involves assigning small, actionable 'quests' across key life domains (Mindset, Health, Wealth, Relationships). Users gain **experience and 'level up'** by completing these real-world actions and submitting verifiable logs (photos, notes). The ultimate goal is to drive real behavioral change, foster a sense of continuous achievement, and measurably increase the user's overall well-being and happiness.

## ğŸ¯ Problem Statement
- **Lack of Actionable Steps:** Existing self-help tools often provide passive content (meditation/reading) but fail to compel users to take consistent, concrete actions in their daily lives.
- **Motivation Decay:** Users struggle to maintain motivation because long-term goals feel overwhelming, and the feedback loop for small daily efforts is often invisible or delayed.
- **Isolation in Improvement:** Individuals working on personal growth often feel isolated, lacking a safe, supportive, and non-judgmental environment to share progress and gain inspiration.

**Goal:** Replace the existing **passive consumption** approach in self-improvement with a smarter, user-centered solution focused on **active, verifiable behavioral change and communal support**.

## ğŸ’¡ Key Objectives
