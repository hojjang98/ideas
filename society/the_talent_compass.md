## ğŸ§ª Technical Approach
1. **Experience Gathering Agents:** Use OAuth2.0 to link accounts and gather data periodically; utilize Webhooks for real-time activity changes.
2. **Creative Value Scoring (CVS):** AI algorithm assigns an objective score based on extracted data. **Metrics include:** **Originality Score** (Novelty vs. existing projects), **Sustainability Score** (Consistency of updates), **Execution Difficulty** (Complexity of tech stack/challenge).

4. **Failure-as-Asset Management:** Scores and visualizes failed projects based on **Execution Difficulty** and **Lessons Learned**, making them valuable inputs for future success.
